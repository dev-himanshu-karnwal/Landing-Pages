Welcome to MediCare Plus

Founded in 1998, MediCare Plus has been serving the community with quality healthcare products and exceptional pharmaceutical services for over 25 years.

What started as a small neighborhood pharmacy has now grown into a trusted healthcare partner for thousands of families across the region. Our commitment to excellence, personalized care, and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

At MediCare Plus, we believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare. That's why we strive to provide affordable medications, expert advice, and compassionate service to all our customers.

Our Mission

To improve the health and wellbeing of our community by providing accessible, high-quality pharmaceutical care and products with integrity, compassion, and expertise.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare partner in our community, recognized for our exceptional service, innovative solutions, and unwavering commitment to patient care.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Compassion, Innovation, and Community. These core values guide our decisions and actions every day as we serve our customers.

Our Journey

A timeline of our growth and achievements

1998

The Beginning

MediCare Plus was founded as a small neighborhood pharmacy with a mission to provide personalized healthcare services.

2005

Expansion

Opened our second branch and introduced home delivery services to better serve our growing customer base.

2010

Innovation

Launched our online platform, making it easier for customers to order medications and access healthcare information.

2015

Healthcare Partner

Established partnerships with local hospitals and healthcare providers to create a more integrated healthcare experience.

2020

Community Focus

Expanded our community health initiatives and launched free health camps and educational programs.

Today

Continuing the Legacy

Continuing to grow while staying true to our core values of providing exceptional pharmaceutical care to our community.
